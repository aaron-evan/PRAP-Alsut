WHO ARE WE?

Bina Nusantara Computer Club Alam Sutera is a technology-driven organization determined to bring about new and creative innovations fit for the current digital era.

BNCC AS was established in 2014, and as of 2020, have been going strong for over five years and will continue to do so for later years to come. Despite being relatively young, BNCC AS can boast multiple successful events such as seminars and workshops that aim to expand people’s knowledge as well as increase their computer skills. The organization wants to create a tightknit community that fosters people’s love for technology and their curiosity for learning more.
